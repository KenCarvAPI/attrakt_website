The Human Layer

Where community becomes culture

Technology connects systems. People connect ecosystems. Shared experiences -- the kind that happen face-to-face, away from screens -- transform scattered individuals into a real community with lasting bonds.

01

Exclusive Retreats

Three days in an inspiring setting. No keynotes, no pitches -- just the most passionate developers building together, sharing meals, and forming bonds that outlast any Slack channel. The relationships forged here become the backbone of a thriving community.

02

Hackathon Experiences

Not another corporate hackathon. Premium, curated events with real mentorship, meaningful prizes, and pathways to grants -- where the best ideas get built and the builders get remembered.

03

Global Roadshows

Taking the community to the world. Intimate meetups and workshops in key tech cities -- connecting local developers to a global movement they want to be part of.

04

Developer Pop-Ups

The unexpected moments that become legends. Surprise activations, immersive experiences, and creative gatherings that spark conversation, define culture, and give developers stories they tell for years.

Every activation is designed around one belief: when developers share a real experience together, they don't just remember the product -- they carry the community forward.
